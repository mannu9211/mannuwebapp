The Great Culture & Heritage of Bihar

Rich Culture of Bihar

Bihar is known for its deep-rooted cultural heritage, folk traditions, and festivals. The state is famous for Chhath Puja, Madhubani paintings, and traditional Bhojpuri music and dance.

Historical Heritage

Bihar has played a significant role in shaping Indian history. It was the seat of the Maurya and Gupta Empires and home to ancient institutions like Nalanda and Vikramshila universities.

Famous Landmarks

Bihar is home to architectural marvels like the Mahabodhi Temple in Bodh Gaya, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
